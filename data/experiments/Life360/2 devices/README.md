# 2-Device Life360 Setups 
## Devices involved: Ultramarine (iPhone 1), Sky (iPad 1), Navy (iPhone 2)

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky checks Ultramarine location every 90 seconds

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Navy checks Ultramarine location once at start, then idle

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Sky location every 2 minutes

- Navy connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Navy location once per minute

- Sky connected to Mac, Navy checks Sky location twice in 5 mins (start + 4 min)

- Navy connected to Mac, Sky checks Navy location every 30 seconds

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky checks Ultramarine location every 3 minutes

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Sky location continuously for 60 seconds, then idle
