# 3-Device Life360 Setups 
## Devices involved: Ultramarine (iPhone 1), Sky (iPad 1), Navy (iPhone 2)

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky checks Ultramarine every minute, Navy checks Ultramarine every two minutes

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Sky every 90 seconds, Navy checks Sky every 2 minutes

- Navy connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Navy every 60 seconds, Sky checks Navy once at start

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky and Navy both check Ultramarine locations, but staggered (Sky at 0:30, 2:30, 4:30; Navy at 1:00, 3:00, 5:00)

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Sky every 2.5 minutes, Navy checks Sky once at middle (2:30)

- Navy connected to Mac, Sky and Ultramarine both check Navy at the same time every 1.5 minutes

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky checks Ultramarine once at start, Navy at end (5:00)

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Sky first 2 minutes (every 30 seconds), Navy last 3 minutes (every minute)

- Navy connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Navy at 1:15, 2:30, and 3:45; Sky checks Navy at 2:00 and 4:00

- Sky connected to Mac, Ultramarine and Navy alternate, each checks Sky every 2 minutes (Ultramarine at 0, 2, 4; Navy at 1, 3, 5)

- Ultramarine connected to Mac, Sky checks Ultramarine every 75 seconds; Navy checks Ultramarine at 2:30 and 5:00

- Navy connected to Mac, Ultramarine checks Navy every 2 minutes, Sky idly has Life360 open but doesn't refresh
